Refractive-index references for the sulfate raw-input data files
================================================================

The optical.sulfate generator builds the 75% sulphuric-acid (H2SO4)
complex refractive index by stitching three published sources onto a
common wavelength grid. Each data file in this directory corresponds to
one source.

ref3_sulfate_table_h2so4_75percent.csv -- broadband background table
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Hummel, J. R., Shettle, E. P. & Longtin, D. R.
"A New Background Stratospheric Aerosol Model for Use in Atmospheric
Radiation Models."
Report AFGL-TR-88-0166, Air Force Geophysics Laboratory (1988).

sulfate_data_s1.txt -- 75% sulphuric-acid index (SRA data)
----------------------------------------------------------
Ferraro, A. J., Charlton-Perez, A. J. & Highwood, E. J.
"Stratospheric dynamics and midlatitude jets under geoengineering with
space mirrors and sulfate and titania aerosols."
Journal of Geophysical Research: Atmospheres 120(2), 414-429 (2015).
doi:10.1002/2014JD022734

sulfate_absorption_ref1.csv -- absorption (imaginary index) measurements
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Gosse, S. F., Labrie, M. & Chylek, P.
"Refractive index of dry and aqueous sulphuric acid: imaginary part of
the refractive index of sulfates and nitrates in the 0.7-2.6 um
spectral region."
Applied Optics 36(16), 3622-3628 (1997).
