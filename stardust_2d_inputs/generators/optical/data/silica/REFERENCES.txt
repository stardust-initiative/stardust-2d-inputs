Refractive-index references for the silica raw-input data files
===============================================================

Data files in this directory:
  n_0-1um.csv, n_1-15um.csv   -- sampled real refractive index n(lambda)
  k_0-1um.csv, k_1-15um.csv   -- sampled imaginary refractive index k(lambda)

These cover the 0-15 um wavelength range and are used by the
optical.silica generator to replace the analytic Kitamura oscillator
model below 8 um.

Source
------
Kitamura, R., Pilon, L. & Jonasz, M.
"Optical constants of silica glass from extreme ultraviolet to far
infrared at near room temperature."
Applied Optics 46(33), 8118-8133 (2007).
doi:10.1364/AO.46.008118
Open-access copy: https://escholarship.org/content/qt3w1982rh/qt3w1982rh.pdf

Notes
-----
The original published oscillator fit was performed against the
7-15 um data and carries a bias in favour of the real refractive
index in that range.
